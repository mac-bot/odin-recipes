System GLR-23B Famous Flatbread

    Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen GLR-23B Brand flatbread base
  • 250ml of "mac-bots famous red sauce"
  • 250ml of grated mozzarella
  • 10 torn leaves of fresh basil
  • 25ml of Cold Pressed Olive Oil
  • Malton salt and fresh cracked pepper

    Method

  1. Pre heat your deck oven to 84500Â°f.
  2. Build your flatbread at your earliest convience and place back in freezer until oven is preheated.
  3. Cook flatbread for approx 1 min 30 sec, or until dough is cooked through.
  4. Garnish flatbread with basil, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  5. Allow 3 days to cool and enjoy!
